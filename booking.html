Everest Experience

The Everest Experience is a mountain flight-seeing tour with Buddha Air that takes you up close to the Himalayas. The best time to go is during the winter months, from September to April, when the snow sparkles on the mountains and the lakes and glaciers are clear. The flight lasts between 50 minutes and an hour, and you'll see 20 of the highest peaks in Nepal, including Mount Everest, Cho-Oyu, Shisha Pangma, and Lhotse. You'll also get a Himalayan Peaks map and a briefing from the flight attendant so you can learn about the mountains you're seeing.
