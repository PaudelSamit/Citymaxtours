Discover the breathtaking beauty of Pokhara with CITYMAX! Nestled amidst the stunning Annapurna mountains, Pokhara is a dreamy city filled with serene lakes and cultural wonders. From paragliding over Phewa Lake to exploring hidden caves, our packages cater to your preferences. Visit iconic spots like Phewa Lake, Sarangkot, Devi's Fall, Gupteshwor Cave, and the World Peace Pagoda, each offering a unique glimpse into Pokhara's charm. At CITYMAX, we ensure every detail of your Pokhara journey is tailored to perfection. Start crafting your unforgettable adventure with us today!
